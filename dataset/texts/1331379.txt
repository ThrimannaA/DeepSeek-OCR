At Intrepid Travel we exist to inspire, create, share and lead the best travel experiences ever — for both people and the planet. More than 34 years later, we're proud to be a certified B Corp with hundreds of trips in over 100 countries. 
As our Team Lead — Product & Reservations (Haka), you'll play a vital role in managing the reservations team that supports Intrepid DMC New Zealand. This includes an understanding of booking systems, coaching team members, overseeing smooth booking processes, all while delivering exceptional service! Beyond day-to-day operations, you'll focus on developing your team's efficiency and performance. With your Reservations expertise, you'll manage the booking and confirmation with suppliers of all accommodation, transportation and required activities on trips in the region. You'll also be the key link between the Colombo team and the New Zealand Reservations team. Whether it's refining booking processes, troubleshooting issues, or driving continuous improvement, your leadership will directly impact the success of our reservations function. 
This full-time permanent position operates in a hybrid capacity out of our office in Colombo, Sri Lanka. 
Some of the experience you'll bring with you may include: 
• Minimum 5 years' experience, preferably with hotel reservations, inbound tour operations and destination management 
• Well-versed with reservations systems, inbound reservations process and handling reservations for inbound tours 
• Experience in handling tour operations and supplier coordination 
• Previous supervisory experience including coaching and developing a team
